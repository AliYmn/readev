Readev Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

Introduction

Readev is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Do Not Collect

Readev does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following types of personal information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Browsing history (except for the current tab's URL)
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • IP addresses

Chrome Extension Permissions

The following permissions are used for specific, limited purposes:

  • Tabs Permission:

    Used only to access the current tab's URL and title for display purposes. This permission is essential for showing the correct content in the new tab page.

  • Storage Permission:

    Used only for storing user preferences locally in your browser, such as:

    • Favorite sites
    • Last selected site
    • Theme preferences
    • User settings

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. We do not share any information with any third-party services or advertisers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
